This Software Developer Salary Guide was created to use machine learning techniques to assist in obtaining a competitive salary offer for resources at Triple Crown Consulting.

The Problem

Hiring in the software development industry is fast-paced, and your company has requested proposals to offer solutions that can potentially enable faster hiring decisions by qualified candidates.

The Solution

The solution enables data-driven decisions, eliminating emotion or stress from the salary negotiation process. This will benefit Triple Crown Consulting team members directly but will also lead to more satisfied new hires being onboarded to work on development project for end clients. The live data product is also sustainable and will not rely on a single individual or maintenance of an internal knowledge base.

The Methodology

The solution is be a browser-based web portal, where employees can login from anywhere and obtain a salary suggestion for a specific software developer candidate. The tool does not require any technical capabilities and will be accessed through an intuitive user interface which will clearly deliver a salary guideline for making an offer.

To use the tool, be sure you have logged in using your Google account. If you dod not have a Google account, you can Create a Google Account.
