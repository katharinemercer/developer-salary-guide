Interactive Salary Area



This area contains interactive query functionality, which can be used to suggest an appropriate salary offer for a qualified software developer candidate in the US.

Use this form to enter information you have gathered from a qualified candidate. Once a candidate has passed interview assessments and reached the offer phase, select the appropriate values below based on the candidate's resume or other interviews you have conducted.

Years of Coding should capture time since the candidate's first hobby or casual exposure to coding.

Years of Coding Professionally should capture time since the candidate first started coding as a primary profession.


For Education Level, use this key:
0 - Never completed any formal education
1 - Primary/elementary school
2 - Secondary school (American high school, etc.)
3 - Some college/university study without earning a degree
4 - Associate degree (A.A., A.S., etc.)
5- Bachelor's degree (B.A., B.S., B.Eng., etc.)
6 - Master's degree (M.A., M.S., M.Eng., MBA, etc.)
7 - Professional degree (JD, MD, etc.)
8 - Other doctoral degree (Ph.D., Ed.D., etc.)
